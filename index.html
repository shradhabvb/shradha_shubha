DESCRIPTION

Established in 2007, we Sheetal Gold are engaged in manufacturing and supplying a wide array of jewelry in Karnataka. The jewelry range offered by us comprises Gold and Silver jewelry, Diamonds and Gem stones, Kundan, Emerald, Ruby and Sapphire, Corporate gifts and BIS Hallmarked Gold Jewelry. In addition to these, our clients can also avail from us semi-Precious stones, Lucky Birth Stones and Gem stones which are certified by GII. The quality ornaments offered by us are manufactured using rudraksh, shaligram, gold and stones that are certified by GII (Gemological Institute of India). Thus, it enables us to provide our clients with flawless range of jewelry. Further, each jewel piece is duly checked by our quality analysts so that only BIS Hall Mark gold (Burro of Indian Standards) is delivered to our valuable clients. Our professionals keep a track of latest market trends, in order to offer fashionable jewelry that attracts women's attention. As we aim towards adding charisma in the women personality, our talented jewelry designers make sincere efforts while designing ostentatious ornaments in gold, silver, pearl and platinum. Also, we hold proficiency in carving stones in the shapes of Elephants, Shree Yantra, Ganesha, Shivling and Buddha Heads. Apart from ruling women hearts, we also provide men's bracelets, rings and chains. We make sure that the clients are provided with international quality standard products that are in compliance with the industry norms. Owing to the efforts made by our jewelry designers and the quality of jewelry offered by us, we have been able to gain the trust of the widespread customers and credibility in the market. Client satisfaction we believe in providing quality assured trendy jewelry to our clients that provides them with maximum satisfaction. Our esteem patrons can avail modern jewelry from us for any occasion as per their needs and requirements. We offer our beautiful jewelry array at market leading prices to our customers. Why us? Over the years, we have been practicing ethical business and making transparent dealings that have gained us a huge clientele base across the nation. Timely delivery, quality jewelry and competitive prices are some of the factors have contributed in our success and to stay ahead of our competitors.
